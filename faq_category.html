1. Can I order Herbalife products from a country that is not in the Baltic Region?

A: While you can place an order from any country in the world using the Herbalife Baltic Online Ordering System, we ship orders only within the Baltic countries (Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania). For Estonia and Lithuania, you can only order products with delivery, while for Latvia, you can also pick up your order from Herbalife Latvia’s Sales Centre.
