Shipping & returns

Shipping Terms
All orders placed by 1pm, shall be processed and delivered within 48 hours on business days on the address indicated by you.
Upon receiving your order from the courier, please inspect the packaging and sign for the delivery.
Once you open the parcel please verify the content with the invoice. Please also inspect your order for any visible external damage.
Deliveries are made through DPD, Omniva, Itella and are charged as follows:

Deliveries for Latvia and Lithuania

Courier delivery Courier delivery (with choice of hour interval) Delivery to DPD (parcel machine) Delivery to Omniva (parcel machine) Delivery to Itella (parcel machine)
10.29 eur* 13.31 eur* 5.38 eur* 5.38 eur* 5.38 eur*
*All prices include VAT(21%)

Prices are not subject to the weight, volume or distance.
Delivery costs are payable on each order.
No delivery costs will be charged for an order containing at least one Herbalife Member Pack.
Deliveries made to parcel machine take place on business days within 48 hours after receipt of payment for the order.

Orders delivered to an parcel machine

  • Best for small to medium orders.
  • Orders will be delivered to an parcel machine located closest to the address indicated in your order. Orders may be picked up at your convenience by you personally or someone else.
  • A parcel code number will be sent to you via SMS. This code will need to be entered into the parcel machine in order to receive the parcel.
  • An order may consist of several parcels. For each parcel you will receive a separate parcel code number via SMS.
  • A parcel is stored in the parcel machine from 3 till 7 days from the time you receive an SMS confirmation regarding the delivery of the order. Any parcels unclaimed within this period will be returned to the warehouse of the sales centre.
  • You may find the location of the Omniva, Itella, DPD parcel machines on theirs websites (www.omniva.lv/lt/ee); (www.itella.lv/lt/ee); (www.dpd.lv/lt/ee).
